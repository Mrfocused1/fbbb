    A reminder that there is 1 week left to submit an award for LTC Awards Dinner 2026.

    The categories that you can enter in are:

    ❤️Wellbeing Champion – this award recognises an individual who has gone above and beyond to support mental wellbeing in their workplace.

    ❤️Excellence in Workplace Wellbeing – Suppliers – this award recognises licensed hospitality suppliers who have developed and implement exceptional wellbeing programmes for their teams.

    ❤️Excellence in Workplace Wellbeing – Operators with under 250 sites- Awarded to an operator with 250 or less sites that has implemented wellbeing programmes that help LTC reach and offer support to their teams.

    ❤️Excellence in Workplace Wellbeing – Operators with over 251 sites- Awarded to an operator with 251 sites or more that has implemented wellbeing programmes that help LTC reach and offer support to their teams.

    ❤️Fundraiser of the Year – Awarded to an individual, group or organisation who took part in, created or hosted fundraising activities to support LTC`s work and went above and beyond to challenge themselves and garner support for the activities.

    Each award celebrates an organisation or an individual that has supported LTC and has championed wellbeing excellence in their organisations, creating, hosting or participating in fundraising activities and extending our reach to support even more people in the industry❤️

    Big news for the LTC JOGLE Pub Run…🥁

    We’re proud to share that Gary McKee MBE, known to many as the “Marathon Man”, will be joining us for the full journey from John O’Groats to Land’s End. 🤩

    Gary is no stranger to extraordinary challenges. After completing 365 marathons in 365 days and raising more than £1.3 million for charity, he’s now taking on every mile of our 900 mile fundraising challenge to help support people across the licensed hospitality community.

    From Monday 13 July to Tuesday 11 August 2026, the LTC JOGLE Pub Run will bring people together across the UK, whether they’re running, walking, volunteering, cheering or supporting along the route.

    It’s a huge challenge with a simple purpose: to raise vital funds so LTC can continue providing practical advice, financial assistance and wellbeing support to those in our licensed hospitality community who need it. 💚

    Thank you to Gary for helping us make every mile matter, and to our supporters Lucky Saint, Avani Solutions, Thatchers Cider, Star Pubs, Punch Pubs and Strava for backing the journey.

    Whether you’re a hospitality business, team member, supplier, runner, walker or supporter, there are plenty of ways to get involved.

    Join the Strava Club and take on the miles virtually, join us for one of our four Community Days or if you have a question, contact us at `info@fbbstrainingacademy.com`

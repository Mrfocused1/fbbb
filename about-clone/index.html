About FBBS Training Academy

Industry-led hair, beauty and wellness education, built around practical skill and real career progression.

Speak to an Educator

Our Story

FBBS Training Academy was founded to give aspiring and established professionals in the hair, beauty and wellness industries a place to train properly — with experienced educators, small class sizes, and a genuine focus on technical skill.

What started as a small studio has grown into a full training academy offering hair, beauty, professional development and one-to-one coaching, supporting students from their very first class through to running their own salon.

Our Mission

Technical Excellence

We teach the practical, hands-on skills the industry actually demands, taught by educators who still work in it.

Professional Confidence

Small group sizes and one-to-one support mean every student gets the attention they need to grow.

Career Progression

From first qualification to salon ownership, our courses are built to support every stage of a career.
